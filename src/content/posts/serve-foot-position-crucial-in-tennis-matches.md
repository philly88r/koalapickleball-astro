Proper <strong>serve technique</strong> demands at least one foot behind the baseline and prohibits any foot from touching the court within or on the baseline. Feet must also steer clear of the <strong>extension lines</strong> of the sideline and centerline.</p>
<p>Once the ball is in play&#44; players have the freedom to move their feet. To master this&#44; engaging in <strong>footwork drills</strong> is essential. By practicing serve footwork diligently&#44; players can enhance their accuracy and ensure compliance with the rules.</p>
