<h2>Control and Power Paddles</h2>
<p>Control and power paddles cater to different playstyles by incorporating distinct materials and designs to either enhance precision and finesse or maximize force and acceleration. <p>On the other hand&#44; power paddles are built for aggressive players who seek to unleash high-speed shots and maximize power. Designed with thinner cores &#40;14 mm or less&#41;&#44; these paddles generate more spin and accelerate the ball&#39;s trajectory further down the court.</p>
