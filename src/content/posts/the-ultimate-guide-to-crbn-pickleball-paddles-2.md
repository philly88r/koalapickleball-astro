<h3 class="wp-block-heading" id="construction-and-materials">Construction and Materials</h3>
<p>The CRBN TruFoam Genesis paddle represents a significant leap in pickleball technology, primarily due to its innovative construction and meticulously chosen materials. Every component is selected and engineered to maximize performance and provide players with an unparalleled experience on the court.</p>
<p>The outer layer composition is a crucial element in the paddle’s responsiveness and feel. CRBN utilizes a combination of carbon fiber and fiberglass, featuring a T700 raw carbon fiber layup. This strategic blend provides an optimal balance of power, control, and durability. The carbon fiber enhances stiffness for powerful shots, while the fiberglass adds a touch of flexibility for increased ball dwell time and improved control.</p>
<p>At the heart of the TruFoam Genesis lies its core material: 100% TruFoamTM. This proprietary foam technology is a game-changer, offering a unique blend of responsiveness and consistency across the entire paddle face. The TruFoam provides exceptional energy transfer, resulting in increased power and a satisfying feel upon contact.</p>
<p>The paddle’s thickness is precision-engineered at 14mm. This specific thickness contributes to the paddle’s overall balance and enhances its maneuverability. The 14mm profile allows for quicker hand speed and improved reaction time, crucial for fast-paced pickleball games.</p>
