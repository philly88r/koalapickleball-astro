<p>The JOOLA Hyperion CFS is a standout table tennis rubber. It&#8217;s made using top-notch rubber tech and careful production. JOOLA focuses on quality at every step. This ensures the rubber gives amazing <b>spin</b>, <b>speed</b>, and <b>control</b> during play.</p>
<p>The making of table tennis rubber is a detailed process. It starts with picking the best raw materials. These include synthetic rubbers and special additives. These are chosen for their stretch, stickiness, and strength.</p>
<p>Next, these materials are mixed well. This mix is vital for the best spin, speed, and control. Special machines blend the materials into a consistent mix.</p>
<p>The mix is then rolled out in a rolling mill. It&#8217;s pressed to the right thickness. This step is key for just the right amount of speed and control. During rolling, the sheet&#8217;s thickness and texture are constantly checked.</p>
<p>Then, the rubber sheet is vulcanized. It&#8217;s heated and pressed, causing the rubber molecules to link and harden. This step boosts the rubber&#8217;s stretch, durability, and overall performance.</p>
<p>After vulcanization, the sheet is finished. It&#8217;s trimmed and coated to last longer and play better. The final sheet is then ready for use in rackets like the JOOLA Hyperion CFS.</p>
