<ul>
<li>🏓 Improve your serve, increase your power, and win more points without losing consistency.</li>



<li>🏓 Using your full body for the serve is crucial for applying pressure to your opponent and maximizing the effectiveness of your serve.</li>



<li>💪 Using your entire body for the serve can maximize power and force, leaving a lot of power on the table if only using your arm or upper body.</li>



<li>🎾 Always transfer your weight and take a step back to get ready for the return in pickleball to avoid being pushed off the line and hitting the ball while on the run or falling backward.</li>



<li>🎾 Serving with pace and depth adds pressure to your opponent and transforms your game.</li>



<li>🎾 Top Spin can create an awkward contact point for your opponents, setting up an easier third shot and creating opportunities for you as you serve.</li>



<li>🎾 Be unpredictable with your serve to keep your opponent guessing and uncomfortable.</li>



<li>🎾 It\&#8217;s important to apply these elements to your game one at a time and build as you go.</li>
</ul>
