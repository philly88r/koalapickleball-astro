<li>🎾 The most important things for hitting a backhand roll in pickleball are swing path and paddle angle to create top spin.</li>

<li>🎾 When hitting the backhand roll in pickleball, it\&#8217;s important to redirect the ball down the line off of a crosscourt ball for the most effective shot.</li>
