<p>Choose Depth Over Speed&#58; Hitting a deep serve return is generally more beneficial than aiming for speed. This puts your opponent under pressure and makes their next shot more challenging&#44; giving you an upper hand.</p>
<p>Drive Forward&#58; Proper transfer of weight to your front foot and powerful hip movement helps to generate more force and trajectory for a deeper return. It also helps to maintain return motion consistency.</p>
<p>Control Your Follow-through&#58; Aim to hit through the ball&#44; increasing your follow-through for a smoother&#44; more controlled motion. This ensures you can maintain power and accuracy in your return of serve.</p>
