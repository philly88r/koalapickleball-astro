<h2 class="wp-block-heading \&quot;wp-block-heading\&quot;" id="\&quot;h-key-insights\&quot;">Key insights</h2>



<ul>
<li>🏓 Beginners need to stop mindlessly following instructions and start understanding the purpose behind each stroke in pickleball.</li>



<li>🏓 Mindlessly driving the ball without dropping accomplishes nothing and makes it easy for opponents to simply block the ball, hindering progress in the game.</li>



<li>🚫 Many players mistakenly think they&#8217;re not allowed to step into the kitchen in pickleball, but you can actually stand in there and dink the ball while standing inside.</li>



<li>🎾 Stop thinking that drives are bad in pickleball, they can actually be effective for your third shots.</li>



<li>🎾 Placement over power is key in pickleball, aiming for the opponent\&#8217;s dominant hip can put them in a difficult position and give you an advantage.</li>



<li>🎾 The key to winning in pickleball is to choose the shot that is most high percentage for you as an individual player.</li>



<li>🎾 The most high percentage shot in pickleball is to hit the ball up the line, giving the opponent less time to react.</li>



<li>🎾 Hitting to the better player and facing weaknesses can lead to improvement and opportunities to play with higher-rated players.</li>
</ul>



<h5 class="wp-block-heading \&quot;wp-block-heading\&quot;">Beginners in pickleball need to focus on understanding the purpose behind each stroke, prioritize placement over power, and choose high percentage shots to improve their game.</h5>



<ul>
<li><a href="\&quot;https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B8xIoRGaa9U&t=0\&quot;">00:00</a> 🏓 Beginner pickleball players need to understand the importance of purposeful strokes, dinking, and dropping the ball to set up for an attack.</li>



<li><a href="\&quot;https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B8xIoRGaa9U&t=88\&quot;">01:28</a> 🏓 Get closer to the net, practice dinking and dropping the ball short to improve your pickleball game and advance to higher levels of play.</li>



<li><a href="\&quot;https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B8xIoRGaa9U&t=244\&quot;">04:03</a> 🏓 Use drives in pickleball for third shots and focus on placement for a good fifth shot drop.</li>



<li><a href="\&quot;https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B8xIoRGaa9U&t=331\&quot;">05:31</a> 🎾 Aim for your opponent\&#8217;s dominant hip to put them in an awkward position and create opportunities for winning shots.</li>



<li><a href="\&quot;https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B8xIoRGaa9U&t=402\&quot;">06:42</a> 🏓 Beginner pickleball players need to focus on hitting the right shots and being in the right position on the court to start winning.</li>



<li><a href="\&quot;https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B8xIoRGaa9U&t=491\&quot;">08:10</a> 🏓 Positioning and strategy are crucial in pickleball, covering respective sides and anticipating opponents\&#8217; shots to increase chances of winning.</li>
