<h2 class="wp-block-heading \&quot;wp-block-heading\&quot;">Key insights</h2>



<ul>
<li>🎾 Hitting crosscourt creates more angle, potentially giving you more windows to hit winning shots through.</li>



<li>🎾 The crosscourt dink has the potential to be a lot more aggressive because it can be a little bit flatter and a little bit harder.</li>



<li>🎾 Crosscourt dinks can create offensive opportunities, while playing to the middle can be more neutralizing or setting up the point.</li>



<li>🎾 The importance of positioning and footwork in pickleball dinking strategy.</li>



<li>🎾 The execution of the shot is difficult, and it\&#8217;s not natural for our body to move in one direction and hit the ball in the opposite direction.</li>



<li>🏞️ When you hit crosscourt, your next coverage responsibility is to get back to cover the middle to help your partner out.</li>



<li>🎾 The safest play in pickleball is to hit the ball to the middle third of the box in front of you.</li>



<li>🏓 Playing the ball in front of you makes it harder for the opponent to get the ball back outside your foot, giving you the advantage.</li>
</ul>
