<h2>Game Restart After Side Out</h2>
<p>Upon losing a rally on serve in <strong>pickleball</strong>&#44; the game restarts on the right side of the court. After a <strong>side out</strong>&#44; the <strong>serving team</strong>&#39;s second player takes over the serve. This transition allows for a strategic advantage as players can plan their next moves based on the new server&#39;s strengths.</p>
<p>It&#39;s essential to communicate effectively with your partner to ensure a seamless handover and maintain a strong <strong>game strategy</strong>. Furthermore&#44; understanding the scoring etiquette is crucial to avoid confusion and maintain a fair play environment.</p>
