<h3 class="wp-block-heading" id="court-reservations">Court Reservations</h3>
<p>Reserving a court at the Arlington Pickleball Club is easy and ensures you have dedicated time to enjoy the game. Our primary method for court reservations is through our online platform, which you can access via our website. Simply create an account, view the court availability calendar, and select your desired time slot. Alternatively, you can reserve a court by calling the front desk during operating hours, and one of our friendly staff members will assist you.</p>
<p>Court reservation fees may apply, depending on your membership status. Please refer to the membership details on our website for specific pricing.
