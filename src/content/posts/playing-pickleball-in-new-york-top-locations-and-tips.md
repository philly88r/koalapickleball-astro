<h2>Learning the Game</h2>

<p>Learn to play pickleball by understanding the basic rules and formats. Familiarize yourself with the court layout&#44; which includes non-volley zones and service lines.</p>

<p>Key aspects to focus on during gameplay include proper stance&#44; footwork&#44; and precise serves and returns. Ensure you know how to score points&#44; particularly on faults and misses.</p>
<p>While the rules are straightforward&#44; honing skills like placing shots out of reach and using angles to outmaneuver opponents will elevate your play.</p>
